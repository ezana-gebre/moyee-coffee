Washing

Washing stations

Like a day in a spa but for cofee beans

Moyee also owns and operates an Organic and RA certified washing station in Anderacha, Sheka Zone where it works with 1200 out-grower coffee farmers who produce over 150,000 KG of specialty coffee per annum. Surrounded by UNESCO heritage sites, our washing stations also contribute to a sustainable environment through water conservation, soil enrichment and integrated weed and pest management. Good for the community, good for the climate!
