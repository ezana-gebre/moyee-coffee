The Delight is in the details

There is nothing quite like seeing the smile on someones face when they taste an exceptionally good cup of coffee. Thats why the final part of our supply chain must involve the final consumer. Within Ethiopia, our packaged coffee is readily available in Addis Ababa at cafes, hotels, embassies, supermarkets. Its already been a hit, but were taking it a step further. We are opening cafés around Addis. In fact, our flagship location is now open in Bole, as well as one within Friendship Park. Several others are opening soon. Internationally, Moyee coffee can be found in the isles of IKEA, MASS, DELIXL, MENY-Denmark and Albert Hein (which is also the owner of Giant Foods in the US). Oh, one last thing: were also designing barista and cupping training sessions! We’ll keep you posted on that, but we think it’s going to be a great way to add even more value to the local coffee experience in Ethiopia.
