Our Coffee

The Way coffee was always meant to be cultivated!

Harvested from some of coffees most famous and ancient origins the forested mountains of Kaffa, Anderacha/ Masha, Sidama, Limu, Jimma, Guji, Yirgachefe and Harrar, our 100% Arabica coffee is unmatched in quality. Forest-grown coffee is known to be the richest in flavor because thats how coffee was always meant to grow. We could have done things in the typical plantation fashion, producing more yield, with a slight drop in quality but who wants a mediocre coffee? Instead, by focusing on quality, and doing so much value addition at the country of origin, were able to provide specialty, single-origin coffee, cheaper than was previously possible. Oh yeah, this is how coffee was always meant to be.
