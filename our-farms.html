Our Farms

Our Farms are forests

Thats why our coffee is so good. For thousands of years, coffee has naturally grown under the shade of forests, in a complex ecosystem of plants and wildlife. So, we figured... why change that? This is why Moyee coffee is grown under the shade of Ethiopias most legendary forests. Our specialty 1000 HA Organic Farms (owned by our sister company Tega & Tula Coffee Farm) are located in the birthplace of Arabica coffee Kaffa and Anderacha. These forests have been organic certified by the Rain Forest Alliance (RFA), USDA, EU and Japanese Agricultural Standards (JAS). But its not just about our farm its also about the farmers. Thats why we make sure the 3000 out-growers we work with are also certified, well paid (20% above market) and their communities are the first to benefit from our work. We reinvest a portion of profits back into the communities through infrastructure development and capacity building, and we offer extensive support to the farms throughout the year... This is where FairChain starts for us, and these communities are the most important part of the whole value chain.
